Messaging angles that work

These are the 3 approaches that consistently get replies in this vertical. Pick the one that fits your prospect best.

1. Website audit
"I looked at {{company}}.com. A few things stood out that might be costing you conversions."
Why it works: Specific observation = credibility. Nobody ignores an email that says their website has problems.
2. Competitor comparison
"Your competitor just relaunched their site. Here's what they changed and why it matters."
Why it works: Competitive pressure + specific intel. The prospect has to click.
3. Industry benchmark
"The average conversion rate in [industry] is 3.2%. Most sites we audit are at 1.5%."
Why it works: Data-driven. Makes the prospect wonder where they stand.

When they push back

The most common objections in this vertical and exactly what to say.

"We're happy with our current agency"
Great. Ask: when was the last time they audited your site performance? Most agencies do the build and move on. We focus on ongoing optimization. Different model.
"We just redid our website"
Perfect timing. Ask: are you tracking conversion rates? Most new sites launch without proper tracking. We can help set up the measurement framework so you know what's working.
