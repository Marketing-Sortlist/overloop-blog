9 Best AI Multichannel Outreach Tools

A review of the 9 best AI multichannel outreach tools, comparing features, pros, and cons.

Nicolas Finet | March 13, 2024 | 10 min read

In this post, we’ll look at 9 of the best AI Multichannel Outreach Tools, focusing on features, pros, and cons.

When looking for an AI Multichannel Outreach tool, you need one that:

  • Finds sales leads at scale;
  • Sources information on those sales leads from multiple channels;
  • Hyper-personalizes messages and email sequences;
  • Conducts outreach sequences across multiple channels.

For B2B sales, demand, and lead gen, the most effective channels are email and LinkedIn.

In some cases, AI multi-channel outreach tools incorporate text/SMS, WhatsApp (when an AI product can source a mobile number), and other social networks, such as Twitter/X.

However, we’d stick with email and LinkedIn as they’re always going to be the most effective for B2B sales campaigns.

Business development reps (BDRs), sales development reps (SDRs), and other demand-gen employees are only as good ⏤ and safe in their jobs ⏤ as their last quarter’s performance.

So, with the emergence of AI multichannel outreach tools, are BDRs and SDRs even less secure in their jobs?

No, is the short answer.

  • AI Multichannel Outreach tools haven’t replaced SDRs and BDRs.

  1. Finding prospects with enough data points to make hyper-personalized outreach easier;

In every way, AI multichannel outreach tools are making sales reps better at their jobs.

What are AI Multichannel Outreach Tools?

AI Multichannel Outreach tools are sales outreach software products (SaaS) that are built to source leads and implement sales campaigns.

Unlike traditional methods for sales outreach, lead generation, and demand gen, AI-based tools save a huge amount of time.

Salespeople and founders don’t need to go searching for sales prospects manually.

Let’s say, to target 1000 prospects every month:

  • Finding them manually could take 15 minutes per prospect, easily, if not longer, especially when you’re hunting for email addresses
  • Then you have to write personalized email sequences
  • That could take another 15 minutes per prospect
  • You need to schedule follow-ups so they go out, otherwise it’s easy to forget.

We’re easily talking 30 minutes ⏤ if not more time ⏤ for every prospect.

So, for 1000 prospects that’s 500 hours . . . that’s 12.5 weeks of work!

Basically, doing this the manual way would take 1 quarter’s worth of work for a BDR. When do you have time for booked sales calls and demos, or anything else?

AI multichannel outreach tools solve all of those problems and save a HUGE amount of time.
