When AI-powered (Artificial Intelligence) agents started coming out, many sales reps ⏤ especially those at the prospecting end started ⏤ worrying about job security.

Business development reps (BDRs), sales development reps (SDRs), and other demand-gen employees are only as good ⏤ and safe in their jobs ⏤ as their last quarter’s performance.

So, with the emergence of AI agents, are BDRs and SDRs even less secure in their jobs?

No, is the short answer.

  • AI Agents haven’t ⏤ and won’t ⏤ replace SDRs and BDRs.
  • We are seeing that with SDRs and BDRs: AI tools like Overloop AI can take 90% of the most difficult, time-consuming, and boring work off a sales reps desk:

In every way, AI agents are making sales reps better at their jobs.
