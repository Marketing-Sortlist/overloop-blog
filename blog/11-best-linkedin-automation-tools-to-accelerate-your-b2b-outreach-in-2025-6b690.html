HR
#2

HeyReach

★★★★★
4.7/5 on G2

HeyReach offers growth teams a practical way to automate and manage multiple LinkedIn campaigns at once. The platform is designed for teams who need to run high-volume outreach while keeping full control and visibility over each campaign. Intuitive dashboards let users track activity, follow up on responses, and understand what works, without constant manual checks.

Users can set up targeted LinkedIn sequences across several accounts, making it easy to reach narrow segments while scaling overall outreach. The system's analytics provide real-time feedback on impressions, connection rates, replies, and meetings booked. These insights support continual improvement without the guesswork, helping teams allocate time where data shows the most potential.

HeyReach lets teams A/B test messages and strategies directly inside each campaign. The platform shows side-by-side results so sales teams see immediately which copy or approach converts best. This feature is especially useful for teams with dynamic value propositions and evolving markets. Teams can optimize scripts before rolling changes out to every lead, improving performance week after week.

LH
#4

Linked Helper

★★★★★
4.5/5 on G2

Teams can build outreach sequences that combine LinkedIn connection requests, messages, InMails, and email steps within a single campaign flow. This removes the need to manage separate tools for each channel and keeps prospect engagement coordinated across touchpoints. Unlimited LinkedIn sender support means agencies and larger teams can scale outreach without running into seat-based pricing walls, making it a practical option for operations that manage multiple client accounts simultaneously.

Agent Frank, Salesforge's built-in AI SDR, takes on prospecting, message writing, follow-ups, and meeting booking autonomously. The system connects to a large contact search engine, identifies relevant prospects, and personalizes outreach based on available data, without requiring constant manual input. For teams looking to reduce SDR overhead while maintaining consistent pipeline activity, Agent Frank operates as a parallel outbound function rather than just a supporting feature.

Salesforge addresses inbox deliverability through smart mailbox rotation, unlimited warm-up, and an ecosystem of infrastructure add-ons via Mailforge, Infraforge, and Primeforge. Sending is distributed across multiple inboxes automatically, lowering the risk of domain damage during high-volume campaigns. Teams with proper domain and mailbox setup benefit most from this layer, as the platform is designed to reward deliberate infrastructure management with stronger inbox placement over time.

Pricing: The Pro plan starts at $48/month and covers 1,000 active contacts, 5,000 emails, unlimited mailboxes, warm-up, and the Primebox unified inbox. Growth, at $96/month, expands to 10,000 active contacts, 50,000 emails, unlimited LinkedIn senders, A/B testing, multi-language sequences, and API access. Agent Frank is available as a separate add-on at $499/month, billed quarterly, and includes dedicated onboarding alongside the full AI SDR feature set. For platform details or to explore available plans, visitsalesforge.ai.

#5

Expandi.io

★★★★☆
4.0/5 on G2

Expandi.io delivers a cloud-based approach that lets agencies and outreach-focused professionals automate LinkedIn prospecting securely and at scale. By running LinkedIn automations fully in the cloud, Expandi minimizes account risk and removes the need to leave a computer running, appealing to users managing multiple client profiles or high-volume campaigns.

Expandi offers flexible sequence builders that let users design, schedule, and launch LinkedIn campaigns tailored to specific lists and targets. Automated tasks like profile views, connection requests, messaging, and follow-up sequences can adapt to prospect behaviors. For agencies, the ability to assign different client profiles and quickly copy proven campaign structures across accounts adds real timesaving and operational consistency.

If your team relies on large-scale outbound sequences, Expandi.io provides an environment focused on safety and scalability. Linking multiple profiles, segmenting outreach, and tracking results are all managed with an eye toward reducing daily admin time. For detailed pricing and a complete list of automation options, visit https://expandi.io/.

MA
#6

Meet Alfred

★★★☆☆
3.4/5 on G2

Sales teams aiming for a united outbound approach often need a tool that handles more than just LinkedIn. Meet Alfred stands out for those building campaigns across LinkedIn, email, and Twitter, letting users coordinate every message from a single workspace. With this multi-channel capability, prospecting no longer feels like juggling disconnected tools or processes.

Users design personalized sequences by dragging and dropping steps such as connection requests, follow-ups, or emails into a visual builder. Each step can send messages to LinkedIn, email inboxes, or even Twitter, all scheduled and managed from one dashboard. The interface is intuitive, so teams can launch multi-touch outreach without technical barriers or steep learning curves.

Meet Alfred helps sales reps create well-crafted messages using AI-powered writing suggestions. This feature takes context from each prospect and generates text that feels personal and relevant, not generic. Team members can review, edit, and approve each message, ensuring every touchpoint fits brand guidelines and individual preferences.

LinkedRadar

★★★★☆

Some sales teams need a quick and accessible automation tool without the complication of setup fees or steep learning curves. LinkedRadar fits this task by offering free, lightweight AI automation features for LinkedIn tasks. SN
#8

Snov.io

★★★★★
4.6/5 on G2

Snov.io appeals to sales teams seeking both powerful LinkedIn automation and an integrated multichannel suite for outreach. Its setup streamlines the process of finding leads, connecting on LinkedIn, and moving seamlessly into coordinated campaigns across channels.

Users can discover verified email addresses directly on LinkedIn profiles, then add those contacts to campaigns with one click. Snov.io supports the automation of personalized connection requests, follow-ups, and messaging within LinkedIn, all managed from the same interface as email and phone outreach. Automated drip sequences and response tracking simplify the engagement cycle, letting users manage LinkedIn touchpoints without losing context or switching tools. This integrated workflow is especially practical for teams targeting prospects on several channels at once.

OC
#9

Octopus CRM

★★★★★
4.6/5 on G2

Octopus CRM remains a top pick for teams that want both automation power and straightforward management of LinkedIn prospects, all without switching between platforms. The tool lets users automate essential tasks such as profile visits, connection requests, messages, and endorsement activities, which saves time and reduces repetitive manual work.

What makes Octopus CRM stand out is its built-in mini-CRM. Users can organize prospects into custom funnels, monitor each lead's movement through the outreach stages, and add notes right alongside every contact profile. Instead of exporting and importing CSVs between different tools, sales professionals keep their outreach data in one place for easy follow-up and tracking.

Safety controls are integrated, including customizable limits that mimic human behavior, which reduce the risk of LinkedIn account restrictions. Real-time dashboard reports show invitation acceptance and reply rates, helping teams refine their approach and get the most out of their prospect lists.

#10

Dux-Soup

★★★★☆
4.3/5 on G2

Dux-Soup gives sales teams an easy way to automate personalized outreach on LinkedIn directly from the Chrome browser. The tool sits as an extension, so users never need to leave their current workflow or open a separate app. Its interface is light but practical, making it popular for those who value quick access and a short learning curve.

PB
#11

Phantombuster

★★★★☆
4.3/5 on G2

Phantombuster stands out for users who want full flexibility across their LinkedIn workflows without building their own code or integrations. The platform operates entirely in the cloud, which removes local installation needs and means your workflows run around the clock, regardless of your device or location.

Sales teams and marketing specialists use Phantombuster for tasks like gathering contact data, collecting posts or profile details, and sending automated messages or connection requests on LinkedIn. The platform offers a library of ready-made "Phantoms"—single-purpose automation bots—that users mix and match to build custom sequences. For example, a common workflow might scrape a search of decision-makers, export connection details, then trigger fully automated, scheduled outreach and follow-up messaging at set intervals.

While largely aimed at tech-savvy teams or those comfortable customizing workflows, Phantombuster also supports simpler use cases with prebuilt templates that minimize setup time. This enables fast prototyping and scaling, especially if your B2B outreach strategies frequently change or require integrating LinkedIn steps into broader automation stacks.
