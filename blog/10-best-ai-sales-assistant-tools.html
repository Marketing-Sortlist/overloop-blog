    Salesloft

    A sales engagement platform with AI sales assistant tools.

    Features:

    • Now known as a “revenue orchestration platform”, built using AI-powered tools
    • Manages the entire sales lifecycle, particularly for SaaS companies
    • Improves the seller workflow with Rhythm, an AI-powered signal-to-action engine

    Pros:

    • A market leader in the Forrester Wave: Sales Engagement Platforms (2022)
    • Lots of features, making it especially useful for larger sales teams

    Cons:

    • Product is priced more for larger (Enterprise SaaS) sales teams, competing with the likes of Salesforce
    • Might have more features than smaller businesses, agencies, and startups want or need, especially for prospecting and demand generation.

    Avoma

    An AI assistant sales platform.

    Features:

    • Tools for AI note-taking and sales meetings
    • Meeting scheduling and booking features
    • Integrations with numerous other sales tools, such as CRMs and video call products

    Pros:

    • It’s a great AI assistant for before, during, and after-sales calls/demos
    • It rivals and replaces other products that do similar functions

    Cons:

    • The only downside is that it’s not a demand/lead-gen AI assistant
    • It won’t get you those sales calls, so it would need to be used alongside a SaaS product like that.

    HubSpot

    HubSpot is a power suite of multiple marketing and sales tools built on their CRM (customer relationship management) software.

    As part of HubSpot’s suite of inbound SaaS tools, it now comes with an AI assistant tool.

    Features:

    • Deal management, reporting, and analytics
    • Supports AI-based outreach
    • Integration with over 1,400 apps and other tools in HubSpot’s App Marketplace

    Pros:

    • One of the most reliable CRMs on the market
    • Integrations and new AI sales tool feature

    Cons:

    • HubSpot doesn’t go into much detail about how the AI features work or if they support automated cold email outreach
    • HubSpot’s pricing is usually geared towards Enterprise-level customers, so can be expensive for small and medium businesses.

    Outreach

    Outreach is a “Sales Execution Platform” for Sales, RevOps, and Go-to-Market Leaders.

    Outreach is an all-in-one sales platform that now includes an AI assistant tool.

    Features:

    • AI sales tools include Smart Account Assist and Smart Deal Assist
    • These help sales reps close more deals faster
    • They also provide more useful insights about buyer intentions and pipeline value

    Pros:

    • A feature-rich platform that seems capable of handling every aspect of sales and lead generation.
    • It will even help coach sales reps to close higher-value deals

    Cons:

    • It won’t automatically run AI-based cold outbound lead generation campaigns. Yould would need a different AI sales tool for that
    • It’s another powerful CRM-based tool; but, it’s designed for leads already in the pipeline

    Postaga

    An outbound cold email platform that comes with AI sales assistant tools.

    Features:

    • AI-powered cold email sequences
    • However, it’s more built for link-building agencies, as that’s what the features advertise

    Pros:

    • Postaga could be used for cold sales email outreach
    • But if you’re a link-building agency, this could be even more useful to secure backlinks for client campaigns

    Cons:

    • No integrations with sales tools and platforms
    • Missing a lot of features that would make it more useful for sales (although it does advertise this as a use case).

    Salesforce

    Salesforce is another CRM powerhouse that’s embraced the AI assistant tool movement.

    With Salesforce, you can automate aspects of your sales cycle with their AI assistant tool.

    Features:

    • Sales AI product automates aspects of outbound and inbound sales with Salesforce’s Einstein Copilot, an AI assistant tool.
    • It’s used to guide sellers (salespeople) to close deals more effectively
    • Gives sales teams more insights with predictive AI tools

    Pros:

    • Salesforce’s Einstein 1 is tailored for once a sales lead enters a pipeline
    • Predictive analytics helps sales teams to realize more value from every deal

    Cons:

    • An AI sales assistant is less helpful when you’re trying to get sales leads from cold outreach.
    • Salesforce’s Sales AI product can’t do that part of the sales campaign.

    Clay

    An AI sales assistant tool for better outreach and lead generation data.

    Features:

    • Scrapes lead data from 75 sources
    • An AI sales assistant enriches those leads
    • Automated tools send outbound sales emails

    Pros:

    • Custom sales data enrichment
    • Sales AI assistant integrations

    Cons:

    • The quality of the data and sources can be questionable, especially if it’s scraping from your own LinkedIn profile and contact details are non-existent or out-dated
    • Number of data sources is more limited than other tools

    InstantlyAI

    Instantly is an AI-powered sales tool.

    Instantly provides an AI assistant tool to help businesses get more clients.

    Features:

    • DFY email setup and automated warmup;
    • A Lead Finder database (or you can use your own);
    • Automated outbound campaigns.

    Pros:

    • A low-ish cost compared to some AI sales tools;
    • 24/7 support team.

    Cons:

    • DFY setup doesn’t work as well as advertised, with higher hard bounce rates than expected;
    • Customer support struggles to keep up with the high levels of support tickets.

    Lavender

    A real-time AI sales assistant cold outreach email tool.

    Features:

    • AI writing tools that can be trained to your tone of voice (ToV)
    • Helps you write cold emails faster
    • Tracks email performance and helps you improve responses

    Pros:

    • Claims to double replies in 50% of the time of other AI assistant tools
    • Improves email responses over time

    Cons:

    • Could slow your browser down when it’s being used
    • Can be distracting in your inbox
