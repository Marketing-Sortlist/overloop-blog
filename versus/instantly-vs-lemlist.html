Instantly: Strengths & Limitations

Instantly offers a lower entry price and DFY email setup with automated warmup. It markets itself as a simple, affordable cold email tool with a 280M lead database. However, user reviews consistently report that the DFY setup produces higher hard bounce rates than expected, and AI personalization requires significant manual editing.

The biggest limitation: Instantly is email-only. No LinkedIn outreach, no multi-channel sequences. And leads/warmup are separate products, making the true cost higher than the headline price.

Lemlist: Strengths & Limitations

Lemlist is the more established tool with stronger multi-channel capabilities. It supports email + LinkedIn campaigns, voice notes, custom image personalization, and a 450M database. It's the better choice for creative, human-centric outreach.

The downsides: technical setup is DIY-only, Lemwarm costs extra on lower tiers, and the platform's AI features were added later rather than built-in from the start.

How They Compare on Deliverability

Instantly's DFY setup promises easy deliverability, but reliability issues undermine this promise. Lemlist takes a more active stance with automated warm-up, inbox rotation, and human-like sending patterns, though you need to be on higher-priced tiers to access these features.
