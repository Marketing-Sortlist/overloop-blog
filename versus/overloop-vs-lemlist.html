Setup & Onboarding

The technical setup of email outbound is one of the most critical steps. Get it wrong, and your emails land in spam from day one. Overloop provides a fully done-for-you (DFY) setup: buy domains, create Google Workspace, and Overloop handles all DNS configuration, warmup sequences, and technical optimization.

Lemlist only provides a step-by-step guide and customer support. If you've never configured SPF, DKIM, and DMARC before, this is risky. The DFY approach with Overloop eliminates this risk entirely.

Email Warmup

Email warmup is essential for outbound sales success. With Overloop, warmup is included at every plan level. Lemlist's warmup feature, called Lemwarm, is only available with higher-priced tiers. At the lowest tiers, you need to pay for it as an add-on, which adds unexpected cost.

AI Capabilities

Overloop was built as an AI-first platform. The AI engine analyzes lead websites and social profiles to craft hyper-personalized emails, subject lines, and follow-up sequences. This is deeply integrated into every workflow.

Lemlist has added AI features to its existing platform, but these capabilities were plugged into a product that existed pre-AI. The integration is less seamless, and AI-generated content may require more manual editing.

Multi-Channel Outreach

Both platforms support email and LinkedIn outreach in multi-channel sequences. Lemlist has a unique advantage with LinkedIn voice notes and custom image personalization, which can boost engagement in certain use cases. Overloop focuses on AI-driven personalization across email and LinkedIn steps.

Pricing

Overloop includes all features in its pricing, with costs based on credits per month. With Lemlist, email warmup and some AI features require higher-tier plans or add-on purchases. For teams that want everything included without surprises, Overloop offers better value.

The Verdict: Overloop wins for hassle-free outbound

If you want DFY setup, included email warmup, and AI-native personalization without paying for add-ons, Overloop is the smarter choice for B2B outreach in 2026.
