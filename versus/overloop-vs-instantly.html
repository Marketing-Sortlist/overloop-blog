Overloop's DFY setup ensures proper DNS configuration (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), controlled warmup sequences, and high deliverability from the start. The domains and email addresses are 100% yours, so there's no vendor lock-in.

Overloop's AI engine analyzes each prospect's website and social media profiles to craft hyper-personalized cold emails and follow-up sequences at scale. Every email is unique and relevant to the recipient.

Instantly's approach to personalization requires significantly more manual inputs and editing. If you're sending thousands of emails per month, this manual work quickly becomes a bottleneck.
